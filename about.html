About This Experiment

Information

This experiment is designed to gather data about participants’ trust decisions. You will be asked to fill out a short pre-survey (including your name and email), then read through 20 scenarios where you choose whether to trust the information or not, then answer 4 questions about each scenario. Finally, you'll be asked to complete a post-survey of 16 questions.

When you finish, your data will be automatically saved to a Back4App (Parse) server.
