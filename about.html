About This Experiment

Information

This experiment is designed to gather data about participants’ trust decisions. You will be asked to fill out a short pre-survey (including your name and email), then read through 20 scenarios where you decide whether to trust the information or not. Finally, you'll be asked to complete a post-survey.

When you finish, a CSV file named after you (e.g., John_Smith.csv) will be downloaded automatically. Please send this file to the researcher if requested.
